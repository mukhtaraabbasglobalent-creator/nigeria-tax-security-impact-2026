# Digital Tax Security & Individual Tax Impact Assessment in Nigeria (2026)

**Author:** Mukhtar Aliyu  
**Role:** Cybersecurity & Digital Governance Researcher  

## Overview
This repository contains a research-based and simulated assessment of Nigeria’s digital tax systems, focusing on:

- Cybersecurity risks affecting digital tax platforms
- Simulated penetration testing case studies
- Individual tax impact analysis under recent fiscal reforms

All security testing demonstrations are conducted in controlled, simulated environments.
No live government systems are tested or interfered with.

## Objectives
- Support protection of national revenue systems
- Improve public trust through secure digital governance
- Clarify individual tax impacts using transparent models
- Encourage collaboration between policymakers and technologists
